About Chef Suliman

Born and raised in Riyadh, Chef Suliman grew up fascinated by traditional Saudi flavors. From a young age, he dreamed of opening a restaurant that offers classic Saudi dishes with a modern touch. His passion for food guided him through years of practice and exploration, until he finally brought this dream to life right here.
